## 1. Public account cover/article title picture

**Specification**:
- Cover image: 2.35:1 (900×383px or 1200×510px)
- Text illustration: 16:9 (1200×675px) or 4:3 (1200×900px)

**Key Design Elements**:
- Prioritize visual impact (users scan quickly in the information flow)
- Little or no text (the official account title will be overlaid on it)
- Moderate color saturation (the WeChat reading environment is white)
- Avoid excessive detail (thumbnails should also be legible)

**Recommended Styles**: 01 Pentagram / 11 Build / 12 Sagmeister / 18 Kenya Hara / 07 Field.io

## 4. PPT / Keynote presentation

**Specification**:
- Standard: 16:9 (1920×1080px)
- Widescreen: 16:10 (1920×1200px)

**Key Design Elements**:
- One core message per page (no stacking)
- Clear font size levels (title 40pt+ / text 24pt+ / notes 16pt+)
- A lot of white space for clearer projection
- Image to text ratio at least 60:40
- Consistent visual system (colors, fonts, spacing)
